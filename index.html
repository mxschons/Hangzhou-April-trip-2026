Stories & Secrets

Trip Trivia

Weird, wonderful, and hilarious stories to make every temple, tea field, and waterway come alive.

West Lake

The lake formed when a dragon dropped a magic pearl

A Jade Dragon and Golden Phoenix polished a magical pearl for years. The Queen Mother of Heaven grew jealous and sent soldiers to steal it. During the cosmic battle, the pearl was knocked from heaven and plummeted to earth‚Äîbecoming West Lake.

Longjing Tea

This tea once sold for more per gram than gold

In 2012, 500g of pre-market Dragon Well sold for 180,000 Yuan. Each kilogram requires 36,000‚Äì120,000 hand-picked buds. The flat leaf shape exists because Emperor Qianlong stuffed leaves in his sleeve while rushing to see his sick mother.

Qiantang River

China invented surfing‚Äîthen banned it for being too cool

The Song Dynasty had professional surfers called "Children of the Tide" who performed tricks for the emperor. The practice was banned because officials thought the tattooed surfers were "arrogant people who neglected their family obligations."

Lingyin Temple

The drunk monk who got expelled is now worshipped as a god

Ji Gong (1130‚Äì1209) drank wine, ate meat, and broke every monastic rule. When the temple needed wood, he slept drunk for three days, then shouted "The wood is here!" Logs mysteriously appeared in a well from 900 miles away.

The Bund

The bronze lions survived WWII by hiding in a comedy troupe's warehouse

The famous HSBC lions "Stephen and Stitt" were nearly melted for weapons by the Japanese. During the Cultural Revolution, they were stored in the warehouse of the Shanghai Comedy Troupe‚Äîsurely history's most amusing preservation strategy.

Yu Garden

The zigzag bridge was designed so ghosts can't follow you

Chinese folklore says ghosts can only travel in straight lines and can't turn corners. The Bridge of Nine Turnings is also believed to be the model for Blue Willow chinaware.

Wuzhen

China's internet summit is held in a town that had no roads until 1985

Tech titans like Jack Ma gather in this 1,300-year-old town that once only had steamboat access. The Sanbai Wine distillery has operated continuously since the 1360s‚Äîthe only surviving winery from the Ming Dynasty.

French Concession

The top gangster was also the Chief of Police

Huang Jinrong ran the underworld while serving as Chief Inspector of the French Concession Police and head of the anti-opium task force. He basically arrested his competition. The Communist Party was founded here in 1921.

Culture Shocks to Expect

Hot Water Obsession

Order water during blazing summer heat and receive boiling hot water. Chinese medicine says cold water is bad for digestion.

The Number 4 is Death

The word "four" sounds like "death." Many buildings skip the 4th floor entirely. 514 in a phone number sounds like "I want to die."

The Number 8 is Gold

Sounds like "prosperity." The Beijing Olympics started at 8:08 PM on 8/8/08.

Napping is Normal

Chinese workers often have up to 2 hours for lunch and it's perfectly normal to nap at your desk.

One Time Zone

Despite being the same size as the continental US, all of China operates on Beijing time.

You Might Become Famous

If anyone in your family is blonde, prepare to be photographed constantly and asked for selfies.
